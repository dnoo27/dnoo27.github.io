The cost of financial distractions

Employers are increasingly recognizing their role in supporting their employees' financial wellbeing-as well as the potential savings and gains they can realize in doing so.

Stress over income insecurity or financial precarity has a business cost: As more workers worry about finances, their productivity and performance suffer, with consequences for employers. What's worse, nearly half of all employees are stressed about finances at work, and more than 40% of all workers say that their level of stress about financial issues has increased over the past year (PwC, 2018. Employee Financial Wellness Survey: 2018 Results).
